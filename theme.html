Conference Themes

The scope of AICRAL 2025 includes areas of Linguistics, Applied Linguistics and Language Practices. Abstracts for papers and posters, for AICRAL 2025 are invited in the following areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence in Language Education
  • CALL and Language Research
  • Corpus Linguistics
  • Digital Tools for Language Research
  • Machine Translation
  • Multimodal Media Studies
  • Computational Forensic Approaches
  • NLP Innovations for Cultural Heritage Collections
  • Language Documentation and Preservation
  • Sentiment Analysis in Language and Communication Studies
  • Language and Migration
  • Education, Research, and Globalization
  • Gender and Equality in Education
  • Inclusive Learning and Assistive Technologies
  • Rethinking Assessment in the Age of AI
  • Curriculum and Instructional Design
  • Ecolinguistics and Climate Change
  • Contemporary Linguistics
  • Diversity, Multiculturalism and Inclusion
